What We Offer

Equine Services

A philosophy rooted in trust, communication, and the horse's natural instincts.

Foundation

True Horsemanship

Serving top professionals and those new to horses, True Horsemanship is a philosophy of working with horses based on the horse's natural instincts and methods of communication, with the understanding that horses do not learn through fear or pain, but rather from using common sense, understanding how horses learn and think, and always considering the horse's point of view.

"When you learn the language of the horse you get to the horse's mind. When you work with the horse's mind you win their hearts." — Donna Blem
